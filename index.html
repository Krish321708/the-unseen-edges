01 Practice
I build interfaces and hardware that feel inevitable — systems where every mechanism, every easing curve, every material choice has been considered, then considered again. The work lives in the millimeters between design, code, and manufacturing.

Approach

Eight years deep in production interface engineering and industrial design. I draw the curves, write the code, and mill the chassis. No handoffs, no lossy translation between design and build. The person who decides is the person who ships.

Engagement

One project at a time, fully embedded. Typical engagements run 8–16 weeks. I take on three to four clients per year. Capacity opens May 2025 — two slots remaining.
